About Me

I am a writer, artist, and educator interested in Chinese and Chinese American history, language, and cultural storytelling. I'm fascinated by how culture is preserved through visual art, community spaces, and personal narratives.

I studied computer science and Chinese at UC Berkeley while pursuing design, writing, and education. I enjoy helping students build confidence in reading, writing, and math through clear explanations and patient support. My experience includes ESL reading tutoring, language arts support, large computer science lectures, and small-group instruction. I’m bilingual in English and Mandarin and enjoy working with students of all ages and backgrounds.
